Meet Maureen Nyaguthii Ndungu

A dedicated community leader, passionate advocate, and your next MCA for Karen Ward.

My Story

Growing up in Langata, I witnessed firsthand the challenges facing our community. From inadequate infrastructure to limited opportunities for youth, I knew that change was needed. With a background in community development and a heart for service, I'm ready to be the voice Karen Ward deserves.

I believe in transparent leadership, accountable governance, and putting residents first. Every decision I make will be guided by what's best for our families, our youth, and our future.

Why I'm Running

  • To bring accountable leadership to Karen Ward
  • To ensure every voice is heard in county decisions
  • To create opportunities for our youth and women
  • To protect and enhance our environment
  • To improve infrastructure and basic services

My Vision for Karen Ward

I envision a Karen Ward where:

  • Every child has access to quality education
  • Youth have meaningful employment opportunities
  • Families have access to affordable healthcare
  • Businesses thrive in a supportive environment
  • Our environment is protected for future generations
