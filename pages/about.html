About Maureen Nyaguthii Ndungu

A Passionate Advocate for Karen Ward

My Story

Growing up in Langata, I witnessed firsthand the challenges facing our community. From inadequate infrastructure to limited opportunities for youth, I knew that change was needed. With a background in community development and a heart for service, I'm ready to be the voice Karen Ward deserves.

I believe in transparent leadership, accountable governance, and putting residents first. Every decision I make will be guided by what's best for our families, our youth, and our future.

My Vision

  • Bring accountable leadership to Karen Ward
  • Ensure every voice is heard in county decisions
  • Create opportunities for our youth and women
  • Protect and enhance our environment
  • Improve infrastructure and basic services
