My Manifesto for Karen Ward

A Promise of Progress, Accountability, and Care

📚 Education & Youth Empowerment

  • Establish a youth skills training center in Karen Ward
  • Partner with local businesses to create internship programs for 200+ youth annually
  • Advocate for bursaries to support bright students from low-income families
  • Create after-school programs and mentorship initiatives
  • Support youth-led community projects and entrepreneurship

🌳 Environment & Green Spaces

  • Plant 10,000 trees across Karen Ward in my first term
  • Implement proper waste management and recycling programs
  • Protect and maintain existing green spaces and parks
  • Promote sustainable urban farming initiatives
  • Address illegal dumping and environmental degradation

🛣️ Infrastructure & Basic Services

  • Ensure all roads in Karen Ward are accessible and well-maintained
  • Install street lighting in all neighborhoods for safety
  • Improve drainage systems to prevent flooding
  • Advocate for reliable water supply to all households
  • Regular maintenance of public facilities and amenities

🏥 Healthcare Access

  • Support local health centers with adequate staffing and supplies
  • Organize regular medical camps and health awareness drives
  • Advocate for maternal and child health programs
  • Promote preventive healthcare and wellness initiatives
  • Ensure accessible healthcare for all residents regardless of income

💼 Economic Empowerment & Small Business

  • Create a business support desk for small enterprises
  • Advocate for market spaces for local traders
  • Provide business training and mentorship programs
  • Support women entrepreneurs through special programs
  • Promote local products and services within the ward

👵 Social Welfare & Community Care

  • Establish a social support program for vulnerable families
  • Create a senior citizens' support network
  • Advocate for persons with disabilities' needs
  • Support community-based organizations doing vital work
  • Ensure inclusive development that leaves no one behind
