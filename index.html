My Priorities for Karen Ward

Youth Empowerment

Skills training, internships, and entrepreneurship opportunities for our young people.

Environment

Protecting green spaces, waste management, and tree planting initiatives.

Infrastructure

Better roads, street lighting, drainage, and reliable water supply.

Healthcare

Supporting local clinics, maternal health, and health awareness programs.

Small Business

Market access, business training, and support for local traders.

Social Welfare

Support for vulnerable families, seniors, and persons with disabilities.
