Our Story

Inspired by the spirit of freedom, Inked Sisterhood was formed to create a safe, exhilarating space for women motorcycle enthusiasts in Kenya. We celebrate the intersection of "Inked" culture and the "Sisterhood" of the road.

Whether you ride a cruiser, a sportbike, or an off-road beast, there is a place for you here. Our mission is to promote safety, skill, and the empowerment of women through motorsports.
